Policies & FAQ

Everything you need to know before and after you order. Tap a section to expand it.

  • Standard cakes and desserts can be cancelled free of charge up to 4 hours before the scheduled delivery slot.
  • Custom and tiered cakes require at least 48 hours' notice for cancellation, as preparation begins in advance.
  • Orders placed through Swiggy or Zomato follow that platform's cancellation window — check the app at the time of ordering.
  • Same-day or express deliveries, once confirmed by the outlet, cannot be cancelled.

  • Refunds are issued for orders cancelled within the allowed window, or where Cake L'amore is unable to fulfil a confirmed order.
  • Approved refunds are processed to the original payment method within 5–7 business days.
  • Orders placed via Swiggy or Zomato are refunded through that platform in line with its standard refund timelines.
  • Partial refunds may apply where part of a large or bulk order was delivered successfully.

  • If your cake arrives damaged, melted, or not as described, contact us within 2 hours of delivery with a clear photo of the item and packaging.
  • Verified damage claims are eligible for a free replacement or a full refund, at your choice, subject to outlet availability.
  • Damage caused after delivery, or from incorrect storage once received, is not covered.
  • For orders through Swiggy or Zomato, please raise the claim through the platform's help section for the fastest resolution.

Cake L'amore collects only the information needed to process your enquiry or order — such as your name, phone number, email, and delivery preferences submitted through our forms.

  • We never sell your personal information to third parties.
  • Information shared for custom cake requests is used solely to prepare your quote and order.
  • Orders placed via Swiggy or Zomato are also governed by that platform's own privacy policy.
  • You may request that we delete your contact information at any time by writing to hello@cakelamore.in.

  • All prices listed are in Indian Rupees (INR) and are subject to change without prior notice.
  • Product images are representative; actual cake designs may vary slightly based on availability of decorations and seasonal ingredients.
  • All items are prepared in kitchens that handle dairy, nuts, and gluten-containing ingredients.
  • By placing an order, you agree to provide accurate delivery information; Cake L'amore is not responsible for delays caused by incorrect addresses.
  • Use of this website constitutes acceptance of these terms, which may be updated periodically.

Are all your cakes really eggless? Yes — every recipe at Cake L'amore is 100% eggless, always.

Do you deliver outside Hyderabad? Currently we deliver only within Hyderabad and Secunderabad, from our seven outlets.

Can I order a custom cake for the same day? Simple cakes may be possible same-day depending on outlet availability — call ahead to check. Custom and tiered designs need 48 hours' notice.

How do I track my order? Orders placed via Swiggy or Zomato can be tracked live within those apps.
