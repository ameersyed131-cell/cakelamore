our story

Baked on a simple idea

That eggless never had to mean ordinary. Cake L'amore started with one kitchen and a recipe book built entirely around cream, cocoa and real fruit — no eggs, no compromise on taste.

The beginning

One kitchen, one promise

Cake L'amore opened its first outlet in Hyderabad with a single, uncompromising rule: every cake would be 100% eggless, without ever tasting like it.

Word spreads

Himayath Nagar & Jubilee Hills

As orders grew across the city, new outlets opened in Himayath Nagar and Jubilee Hills — bringing fresh-baked cakes closer to more homes.

Going citywide

Kondapur, Nizampet, Manikonda & beyond

Cake L'amore expanded to Kondapur, Nizampet, Manikonda and LB Nagar, becoming a familiar name for birthdays and celebrations across Hyderabad.

Today

Seven outlets, one standard

With seven outlets spanning Hyderabad and Secunderabad, Cake L'amore continues to bake every single cake fresh, eggless, and by hand.

why eggless

A recipe built for everyone

In a city where so many celebrations call for a purely vegetarian table, we wanted a cake that never asked anyone to compromise. Every recipe at Cake L'amore was developed from scratch to be eggless — not adapted, not substituted.

The result is a cake that's just as soft, just as rich, and just as celebratory as any other — because that was always the whole point.
